About

Ever since my first encounter with cybersecurity an incident with my very own family, I've been captivated by the intricate world of digital defense. This led me to pursue a Master's in Cybersecurity at RIT, where I've honed my skills in incident response, threat hunting, malware analysis, and more. With a solid foundation in Information Technology from Pune University and hands-on experience from roles in global cybersecurity teams, I've dedicated myself to building a robust skill set that bridges both offensive and defensive security. My journey in cybersecurity is built on a passion for understanding evolving threats and a determination to stay ahead of adversaries. During my time as a Security Analyst at Tata Consultancy Services, I became adept at monitoring and responding to real-time security events across a global infrastructure of over 20,000 endpoints. As an intern at PKF Algosmic Cybersecurity, I developed an automated incident response tool in Python that interfaces with ELK SIEM and VirusTotal. This project demonstrated my commitment to efficiency and my ability to leverage programming to reduce manual workloads for security analysts. Beyond academic and professional pursuits, I'm actively involved in hands-on cybersecurity challenges, solving blue-team labs, and sharing insights through blogs on platforms like The DFIR Report. My ultimate goal? To continue to grow my skill in Cyber Defense, specifically towards Threat Hunting and Detection Engineering and eventually towards the offensive side.
